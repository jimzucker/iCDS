iCDS — Option A (semantic split) with cash-emphasis variants

All three phones use Option A's color rules (orange = input, white = output, yellow = headline). Same Calc-tab state: NA / 5Y / 100 bp / 10M / 1000 bp distressed. The Accrued + Upfront Fee row varies — they are the cash components that compose Dirty Upfront, so giving them visual weight helps the eye verify the split.

Option A baseline — no special cash treatment

Accrued and Upfront Fee render at the same size as Start/Maturity (13pt). Easy to scan, but they look identical to less-important date cells.
